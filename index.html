A Little About Me

I'm an attorney and partner formerly @GruberScatchell now @pgsIP, Co-Founder and web developer/designer @StrategiaInc living in Chicago, IL. I'm licensed to practice law in Illinois & California and began my practice in 2013, web development in 2011 and have worked for a wide range of clients.

I have a JD & LLM in Information Technology & Privacy Law from the John Marshall Law School, Chicago. At Pasky Gruber Scatchell my practice focuses on transactional IP & other IP related issues, information technology, privacy, data security, marketing, advertising, & entertainment law.

At Strategia I can guide you through the early stages of project planning and research, help you with information architecture and user experience, design your website, and finally build the site for you. I offer a complete web solution to my clients as well as seek strategic partnerships and invest in new businesses ranging from tech starts up and real estate to restaurant concepts.
